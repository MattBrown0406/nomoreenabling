# No More Enabling SEO Growth Plan

Goal: make No More Enabling the search-driven education property for families affected by addiction, then use that traffic to support ethical advertising, sponsorships, and cross-promotion into Sober Helpline, FamilyBridge, and Freedom Interventions.

## Positioning

No More Enabling should own the emotional-search layer:

- "am I enabling my adult child"
- "how to stop enabling addiction"
- "boundaries with addicted loved one"
- "codependency with addicted spouse"
- "what to do when my son keeps relapsing"
- "how to help without enabling"
- "family addiction support"
- "when is it time for an intervention"

Sober Helpline should own support and community. Freedom Interventions should own premium intervention intent. FamilyBridge should own software/accountability intent.

## Site Architecture

Keep the site focused around topic hubs:

- `/topic-hubs/enabling`
- `/topic-hubs/boundaries`
- `/topic-hubs/codependency`
- `/topic-hubs/family-dynamics`
- `/topic-hubs/intervention`
- `/topic-hubs/recovery`

Each hub should become a pillar page with:

- 1,200-2,000 words of unique guide content
- 8-12 ordered supporting articles
- one quiz, checklist, or downloadable resource
- internal links to Sober Helpline, FamilyBridge, and Freedom Interventions only when the reader's intent is ready
- one clear advertiser-safe category label

## Advertising Readiness

Before selling sponsors at meaningful rates, package the audience around intent, not raw volume.

Suggested products:

- Topic hub sponsorship: one sponsor per hub
- Article category sponsorship: enabling, boundaries, treatment, recovery
- Newsletter sponsorship: one short sponsor block per send
- Podcast + article bundle through Party Wreckers
- Provider spotlight profile with no treatment-placement guarantee

Ethical rule: sell visibility and education placement, not referrals, placement, or clinical recommendations.

- Month 7-9: refresh pages ranking in positions 5-20

## Core Conversion Path

Every high-traffic article should offer one of these next steps:

- take the helping vs enabling assessment
- read the family support guide
- join Sober Helpline
- explore FamilyBridge
- request guidance from Matt
- contact Freedom Interventions for high-risk intervention situations

The rule: No More Enabling earns trust first, then routes readers by need.
